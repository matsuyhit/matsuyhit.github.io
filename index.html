Biography

Hitoshi Matsuyama is a Ph.D. student studying information and communication engineering at Nagoya University. His research interest lies in HCI and Ubicomp, especially for sports/dance analysis and learning. DII Collaborative Graduate Program for Accelerating Innovation in Future Electronics, Ministry of Education adopts him as the 1st-batch student of the program. He is currently working on the dance x XAI project at the National University of Singapore.

Research Interest

1

Activity recognition with ML/DL using multi-modal sensors

2

Dance/Sports analysis with pose estimation

3

Action quality assessment
