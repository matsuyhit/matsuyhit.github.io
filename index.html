Biography

Hitoshi Matsuyama is a Ph.D. student studying information and communication engineering at Nagoya University. He has started up a novel research on ballroom dance performance recognition and evaluation. Also adopted by DII Collaborative Graduate Program for Accelerating Innovation in Future Electronics, Ministry of Education.
